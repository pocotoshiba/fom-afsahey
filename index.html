A Vision to Make the Desert Green Again

Fom Afsahey is a vision brought to life by Lahcen, an 88-year-old farmer who transformed empty desert land into a living oasis. Over the past 30 years, he has planted over 800 trees, mainly olives and palms. Today, this project invites people from around the world to contribute, plant, and be part of a mission to make the desert green again.
