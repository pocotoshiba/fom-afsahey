A Dream to Make the Desert Green Again

Meet Lahcen, the heart and hands behind FOM AFSAHEY.

My name is Lahcen, and I am 88 years old.

Thirty years ago, this land was nothing but dry soil and silence, no trees, no water, just rocks and dust. But I had a dream. Together with my brother, we started this farm from nothing. We dug, planted, prayed, and waited. Slowly, this place came to life.

I started with one water well, but it wasn't enough. So I dug a second one, still, not enough. Then I received a grant from the government to buy a solar pump so I could bring more water from underground. But I still need more batteries for it to work properly.

The land itself is tired. It needs care. It needs a tractor to help make it fertile again, to turn the soil and give life another chance.

Despite the challenges, I have not given up. Afsahey remains a place of hope and now, I invite you to be part of it.

Bring your own tree, plant it in this sacred soil, and watch it grow.
